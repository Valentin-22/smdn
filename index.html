Frequently Asked Questions

Why was I billed?

Smodin is a subscription service providing AI tools for writing and research. Users can cancel their subscription at any time.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription, login to your Dashboard, navigate to Account & Subscription and follow these steps:

  1. Click the pencil icon under Connected Account(s).
  2. Choose "Change" in the Subscription field.
  3. Select "Cancel My Account" in the lower right corner and choose your desired action.
  4. Remember to hit "Confirm".

How do I update billing information?

To adjust the financial accounts you have on file, login to your Dashboard, and navigate to the Account & Subscription section. Follow these steps:

  1. Click the dropdown arrow next to your username (upper right-hand corner).
  2. Select "Account & Subscription".
  3. Click the pencil icon under the "Connected Account(s)".
  4. Choose "Change" in the "Payment" field.

If you need further assistance, please email us your questions.
